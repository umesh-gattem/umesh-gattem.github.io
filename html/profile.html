About

Hi, I'm Umesh Kumar an enthusiastic coder and designer passionate about learning new technologies and its applications. I have always been fascinated by Coding, Mathematics, Algorithms. My keen interest in Mathematics made me one among the toppers of my class during my Secondary Education. I have participated in so many Maths Olympiad Examinations and got some scholarships. Later I joined M.P.C (Maths, Physics, Chemistry) group in my Intermediate Studies and then by giving a common entrance examination, EAMCET (Engineering Agricultural and Medical Common Entrance Test) where I got a good rank and secured a seat in the “Computer Science Engineering” department in prestigious Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Science College, affiliated to Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, India.


In four years of my Bachelor’s degree, I acquired an overall perspective of this scientific discipline, with a particular interest in Coding and Programming, Object-Oriented Programming concepts Database Management, Data Structures, etc. I have participated in so many online Coding competitions like CodeChef, Hackerank and I really enjoy the delightful feeling I get after solving a difficult problem. I have been awarded 3rd prize in “Code Drift”, a coding competition held at our institution. For my final year dissertation, I have worked on the project “Dynamic Resource Allocation using Virtual Machine Migration in IaaS” leading a team of four members. As part of my Summer Internship, I have worked on “Android Application” on my college database which consists of features like attendance of students, faculty details, library details, transportation facilities leading a team of four members. Till then my coding had been largely restricted to basic C++, these projects have forced me to work on Java, HTML, CSS and I readily accepted it.


With a solid foundation in the field of computer science, I have attempted and cleared so many interviews and placed in Infosys Private Limited and Capgemini companies. But with the interest in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, I have searched for AI startup companies. Finally, in July 2016 I joined Razorthink Technologies Private Limited which is one of the top AI startup companies in India. I joined as a python software developer and later moved to the Deep Learning team where I started working on Tensorflow. In these three years, I have been part of the Razorthink AI(RZTAI) platform product team which builds AI solutions for the end-users. I am one of the four developers who contributed deep learning capabilities to the RZTAI platform. We have built a deep learning Framework called Razorthink Deep Learning(RZTDL) which is built on top of Tensorflow, consisting of API’s for training, inferring deep learning models. I have been a major contributor to the RZTDL framework where I implemented several Tensorflow operations like Recurrent Neural Networks(RNN’s), Feed-Forward Networks, etc. During my work experience, I have gone through some research papers like “A Style based generator architecture for Generative Adversarial Networks'', which propose an alternative generator architecture for GAN’s, borrowing from style transfer literature, “A probe towards understanding of GAN and VAE models”, which compares Vanilla Gan, Wasserstein GAN and Variational Autoencoders, “Self Normalizing Neural Networks(SNN)”, “Adaptive Structure Learning of Artificial Neural Networks”, which presents new algorithms for adaptively learning artificial neural networks and etc.


Being working as an AI Engineer, I have a better understanding of Deep Learning, Neural Networks, and AI Frameworks. With the keen interest on Research and Data Science technologies I decided to do my Master's in the Artificial Intelligence or Data Science field to enhance my skills. I got an offer from “Indiana University Bloomington(IUB)” which is one of the top universities in the United States and is a community-engaged university. Indiana University Bloomington is the flagship campus of IU's eight campuses, known for its innovation, creativity, and academic freedom. Currently I'm pursuing my Master's degree in Data Science Program at IUB.
